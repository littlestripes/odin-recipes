egg pie

a filipino delicacy!

egg pie is a traditional filipino dish, held in high regard by local shamans and waste management consultants. you wouldn't want to be caught dead eating anything other than egg pie if you ever find yourself in the philippines! read on to find out how you! can participate in this storied tradition...

ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 14 oz condensed milk (1 can)
  • 24 oz evaporated milk (2 cans)
  • 2 premade pie crusts
  • 1 tsp bourbon vanilla extract

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. mix the egg yolks in a mixing bowl, then mix in the condensed milk well.
  3. mix in the evaporated milk and the vanilla extract.
  4. strain the mixture into the 2 crusts. bake for 30-45 minutes, or until a bubbly golden layer develops on top. leave in refrigerator to cool.
